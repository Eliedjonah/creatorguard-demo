Detection vs Verification

Detection-Based Systems
  • Analyze patterns after content appears
  • Produce risk scores
  • Can trigger false positives or false negatives
  • Reactive: “This might be fake”

Result: Probable risk signal.

CreatorGuard™ Verification
  • Binds verified identity to origin
  • Creates audit-ready verification records
  • Supports platform enforcement and licensing
  • Authoritative: “This is verified from this identity”

Result: Verifiable ownership signal.

Source-to-Screen Verification Architecture

CreatorGuard™ does not rely on guessing whether content looks fake. It verifies whether content can be traced back to a trusted identity and origin record.

1. Identity Anchor

Binds verified creator identity to digital content at origin.

2. Origin Record

Creates a tamper-evident verification record for audit, licensing, and enforcement.

3. Reconstruction

Checks whether content still connects to its verified origin after reposting, compression, or modification.

4. Enforcement Signal

Verified content becomes trusted. Unverified content becomes a liability signal.
